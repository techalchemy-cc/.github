# TechAlchemy

**Demystifying AI — one simple guide at a time.**

TechAlchemy exists for the curious beginner.  
For the employee who still prints every document.  
For the teacher who heard about ChatGPT but never tried it.  
For anyone who feels left behind by the AI conversation.

We’re building learning resources that assume zero prior knowledge, because technology should be a bridge — not a wall. Clear explanations, plain language, and practical examples that actually make sense.

No jargon. No ego. Just tools and understanding, hand in hand.

👋 _Tech-savvy folks are welcome to lurk, but this space is built for everyone else._
